This is our story
為因應高齡社會來臨，強化社區初級預防功能，降低長者衰弱及失能風險，維護長者日常生活之獨立自主，降低長者的依賴程度，並提供具實證基礎之介入課程。課程設計依據文化照顧的精神與文化敏感度，作為重要的核心概念，設計具有文化合適性的健康介入方案，促使個人、家庭及部落賦能，強化自我健康管理能力，在部落醫療資源無法充裕的條件下，打造文化關懷的長者友善社區，邁入健康自主尊嚴的老年生活，促進活躍老化社會。
In order to respond to the advent of an advanced society, strengthen the primary prevention function of the community, reduce the risk of degeneration and disability of the elderly, maintain the independence of the elderly in daily life, reduce the dependence of the elderly, and provide empirical basis intervention courses. The curriculum design is based on the spirit of cultural care and cultural sensitivity, as an important core concept, designing a culturally appropriate health intervention program to promote the empowerment of individuals, families and tribes, strengthen self-health management capabilities, and have insufficient medical resources in tribes. Under the conditions, build a friendly community of elderly people with cultural care, step into a healthy and dignified elderly life, and promote an active aging society.
